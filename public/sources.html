Calculation Methodology

How estimates were generated: Construction costs are calculated using regional averages from multiple sources, adjusted for:

  • Square footage: Base costs scaled to 1,600 sq ft single-story
  • Finish grade multiplier: Budget (0.85x) ‚Üí Standard (1.0x) ‚Üí Mid-High (1.15x) ‚Üí High-End (1.35x) ‚Üí Luxury (1.6x)
  • Location factor: 1.15x for coastal Oregon (higher material transport costs, weather delays, limited contractor availability)
  • Specific features: Metal roof, wood framing, helical piles, 2.5 bath, wood floors/ceiling

Finance calculations: Standard amortization formula for monthly P&I. Property tax estimated at 1.0% of value annually. Insurance estimated at $300/month for coastal property. PMI rates based on typical ranges for LTV > 80%.

Data Currency

All cost data reflects 2025-2026 estimates. Construction costs can fluctuate significantly based on:

  • Labor availability in coastal Oregon
  • Seasonal construction demand
  • Interest rate changes
  • Local permit and inspection fees
